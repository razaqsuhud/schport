HALIRU DANTORO MEMORIAL COLLEGE OF HEALTH SCIENCE & TECHNOLOGY NEW- BUSSA
in collaboration with JUDES college of Health and science Technology is a private –tertiary institution with the aim of training paramedics. The college was established in 2019 and it is a residential, non-residential and co –educational, to provide health expertise with the objective of providing training and capacity building meaning to uplift the primary, secondary and tertiary healthcare delivery, especially in the Northwest zone and Nigeria as a whole.

OUR MISSION
The college is committed to set up and provide the intellectual, personal, and professional growth of its student, faculty, staff and community in the health sector (s).

OUR VISION
To enrich the community to grow as a society of learners through well designed educational pathways: leading to further education, rewarding careers, cultural empowerment, economic –technological development and entrepreneurship in health sector, to assist and in meeting the MDGs.

CORE VALUES
Learners centred community:- The college is dedicated to providing affordable lifelong objective learning opportunities that foster student success, and empowerment . Mutual respect :-at all times, the college will uphold the dignity of each individual by being ethnical, respectful, fair and courteous in communication and actions. Excellence :- the college maintain high expectation of imparting knowledge and upholds itself accountable for promoting continuous improvement and continued education. Diversity :- the college promotes, celebrates and embraces differences, including cultural and ethnic diversity and diversity of thoughts. Collaboration :- the college fosters innovative, enriching partnerships within the college community and among others that serves the region in encouraging community participation. Innovation – the college is a forward thinking institution that explores creative approaches for the future. Public trust:- the college honours and upholds its commitment to the community through integrity of actions and efficient use of resources.
