Welcome

Haliru Dantoro Memorial College of Health Science & Technology New-Bussa in collaboration with Judes College of Health and science Technology is a private –tertiary institution with the aim of training paramedics. The college was established in 2019 and it is a residential, non-residential and co–educational, to provide health expertise with the objective of providing training and capacity building meaning to uplift the primary, secondary and tertiary healthcare delivery, especially in the Northwest zone and Nigeria as a whole.

The college is committed to set up and provide the intellectual, personal, and professional growth of its student, faculty, staff and community in the health sector (s).
